## MegaClient 1.8.1 Update Log

- Improved launch protection to greatly reduce false detections on legitimate Minecraft mods.

- Normal mods are no longer blocked simply because they mention another client in compatibility information, dependencies, descriptions, or internal files.

- Addons and compatibility mods with similar filenames are now handled more accurately.

- Blocked modifications now require a clear, high-confidence identity match before Minecraft is stopped.

- Improved security messages so it is easier to understand exactly why a file was blocked.

- Updated protection status text throughout the launcher for greater clarity.

- MegaClient client version remains **0.11.11** for **Minecraft 26.2**.
