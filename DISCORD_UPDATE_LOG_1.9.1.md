## MegaClient 1.9.1 Update Log

- Updated the built-in MegaClient client to **version 0.12.1** for **Minecraft 26.2**.

- Reworked the **3D cape preview** so cape artwork is displayed on the correct side and no longer appears reversed or incorrectly mapped.

- Improved cape shape and positioning with a smoother, connected curve behind the player model.

- Improved support for **classic and slim skins**, including more accurate arm textures.

- Improved support for higher-resolution Minecraft skin and cape textures.

- Updated cape cards so they show the actual cape design instead of the complete texture file.

- Reduced preview memory, GPU, and interface usage when the cosmetics page is not visible.

- Improved preview rotation, zooming, loading states, and general responsiveness.

- Included additional small performance and stability improvements throughout the launcher.
